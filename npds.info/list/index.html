NPDS Mailing List

About The List

The mailing list is the place to discuss the installation, use and troubleshooting of NPDS. For example, it’s a good place to ask for help, exchange opinions, request features or generally air your discontent because all this stuff doesn’t turn your Newton into a coffee maker. It’s also the preferred venue for the discussion of future NPDS development.

The NPDS mailing list runs on Ecartis, in case you’re interested in that sort of thing.

How To Subscribe

To subscribe to the mailing list, send an email to with ‘subscribe’ in the subject line.

How To Unsubscribe

To unsubscribe to the mailing list, send an email to with ‘unsubscribe’ in the subject line. We’ll be sad to see you go. Really.
