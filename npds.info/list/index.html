NPDS Mailing List

About The List

This mailing list is a venue for discussing the installation, usage, and troubleshooting of NPDS. Feel free to ask for help, exchange tips, request features, or air your grievances. It’s also the preferred venue for the discussion of future NPDS development, should that ever occur.

For more general discussions about the Newton, check out the NewtonTalk mailing list.

The NPDS mailing list runs on Ecartis (formerly Listar).
