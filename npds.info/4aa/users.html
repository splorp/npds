Meet The Users

Brent Deverman

Brent sure loves his Newton(s). He's fluent in Chinese so he has Chinese input and display on his MP2100 (which, by the way, he took to China with him recently). He's super-active in student government at his University and uses his dedicated Newton server to keep people posted on his comings and goings as well as any news he wants to put up. Brent's system has been a testbed for many, many versions of NPDS 2.0x and has contributed a huge number of great ideas to the NPDS effort so if you get a chance, drop by his 'pad and thank him for his work and Newton evangelism.

Peter Rand

Peter is a translator from Vienna, Austria. He's provided a lot of ideas for NPDS during its development cycle and he's a rabid Newton enthusiast. From my correspondences with Peter, I have gained new momentum in NPDS' development since he pointed out how well Newtscape and NPDS could work together. Thanks Peter!
